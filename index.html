Welcome to Silver Star

A grand new address for unforgettable celebrations in Amravati

Built on Rahatgaon Road, Silver Star brings everything a celebration needs under one roof: an 8,000 sq ft air-conditioned banquet hall, a breezy terrace lawn, a sound-proof DJ hall and 50 luxury rooms for your guests.

We are also home to Amravati's first over-pool bridge, a striking centrepiece that makes every event here feel special. From intimate functions to grand weddings, our spaces and hospitality ensure your guests leave talking about it for years.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Silver Star banquet hall located? +
Silver Star is on Shegaon-Rahatgaon Road, near Late Babasaheb Charjan Marg, Rahatgaon, Amravati, Maharashtra 444602, easily reachable from across the city, with on-site parking.
What is the guest capacity? +
Between our AC hall, open-air lawn and poolside deck, we comfortably host intimate functions as well as grand celebrations of up to 1500+ guests. Tell us your headcount and we'll suggest the ideal space.
Which events can I host here? +
Weddings, receptions, sangeet & haldi, engagement and ring ceremonies, birthdays and anniversaries, plus corporate events and conferences.
Do you provide catering and decoration? +
Yes, multi-cuisine catering, plus stage décor, lighting, DJ and sound can all be arranged. You're also welcome to bring your preferred caterer or decorator.
Is parking available? +
Absolutely. We offer spacious, secure on-site car and two-wheeler parking for your guests.
How do I check dates and book? +
Call or WhatsApp +91 77449 48027, email silverstar.reservations@gmail.com, or use the enquiry form below. We'll confirm availability and arrange a venue visit.
